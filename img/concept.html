The Hike Spot Concept

The concept behind hike spot is pretty simple. You find yourself in an unfamiliar place with an insatiable desire to hike. How do you find the nearest hiking locations and local weather conditions?

  • HIKE SPOT allows a user to search for the nearest hiking trails by inputting their current location. A built-in weather app details the current weather conditions.
  • When traveling, you might not be familiar with local hiking locations. HIKE SPOT is here to help!
  • AS a person who loves to travel and hike in new places, I WANT an app/website that I can visit to discover hiking/trails SO THAT I can find local hiking locations and be aware of the current weather.
